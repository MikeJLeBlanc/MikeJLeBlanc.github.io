PyDew Valley :)

MOVEMENT:

UP - W
DOWN - S
LEFT - A
RIGHT - D

CHANGE TOOL - Q
CHANGE PLANT TYPE - E

USE TOOL - SPACE
PLANT CROP - R

INTERACT (Bed and Trader) - ENTER

You can chop trees down and collect the apples with the axe
Plants will not proceed to the next growth stage without water
Interact with bed to proceed to the next day
Seeds can be bought at trader
Harvest can be sold at trader

No Saving, this is just a fun little project! :)
